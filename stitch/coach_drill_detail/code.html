Diamond Weave Agility

A high-intensity footwork drill designed to improve lateral explosive power and rapid change-of-direction mechanics for elite midfielders.

Category
sprint

Technical Agility

Duration
timer

15 Minutes

Difficulty
signal_cellular_alt

Elite / Advanced

Intensity
bolt

High (90% Max)

Step-by-Step Execution

01

Initial Setup

Place four cones in a diamond formation, 5 yards apart. Position the athlete at the base cone facing the center.

02

The Cross-over Phase

Sprint to the center hub. Execute a low-gravity lateral shuffle to the left perimeter cone without crossing feet.

03

Explosive Pivot

Touch the cone and explode 180 degrees towards the opposite perimeter cone. Maintain eye contact with the coach for reactive signals.

04

Finish & Recovery

Back-pedal to the start position. 30 seconds recovery between sets. Perform 6 total repetitions.

psychology

Coaching Points

  • check_circle Maintain a low center of gravity throughout the lateral transition.
  • check_circle Eyes should be up, scanning the field, not looking at the cones.
  • check_circle Emphasize the "push" from the outside foot during the explosive pivot.
