# **Client's Request**

“We need an intuitive Salesforce CRM sales management platform design that simplifies lead tracking, automates workflows, and provides real-time analytics, helping sales teams close deals faster and improve performance with actionable insights.”

# **About Project**

Salesforce CRM helps sales teams streamline their workflow by providing comprehensive insights into business performance, tracking key opportunities, managing accounts, and forecasting revenue. It enhances decision-making with real-time data and analytics.

# **User Challenges**

Sales teams often struggle with disorganized pipelines, lack of visibility into key accounts, and inaccurate sales forecasts. Managing multiple data points across various stages can be time-consuming, leading to missed opportunities and inefficient decision-making.

# **Our Solutions**

Our design solutions focus on creating an intuitive dashboard with streamlined navigation, clear data visualization, and automated tracking. We enhanced user workflows with organized sales pipelines, real-time insights, and easy access to key performance metrics.

# **Client's Feedback**

“We are planning to expand the platform with adaptive versions for different user needs, ensuring seamless access across devices and deeper integration with third-party tools to enhance sales efficiency and data management.”
